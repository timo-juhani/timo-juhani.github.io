This morning the stability of Internet services had and have been degraded. At the moment of writing it's unclear what's going on. Thus I'm only reporting what we are seeing at the moment. Hopefully, the root cause of the issues can be reported soon. 

During the last +24 h Internet has suffered from a mass of network outages. Prefixes i.e. IP subnets of global (mainly) western companies have disappeared from BGP tables of major internet service providers like TeliaNet, Telecom Italia, Hurricane Electric, China Telecom etc. Companies impacted are Microsoft, Amazon, Cisco, Salesforce, finance sector, Google as examples just to highlight the significance. 

New network outages pop up every 30-60 min all around the world according to ThousandEyes. The firms affected are the same ones however, the service provider that loses prefixes seems to change. Although network outages are common throughout any day the observed volume and spread is abnormal. 

It's impossible to say whether this is a major coincidence with a logical explanation or something malicious tied to the current security situation of the world.
